Pooled Breast Cancer Medication & Longitudinal lab Results

This application shows an example of how clinicians can group patients with a specific SNOMED ID, and find their medication information as well as relevant lab results.

This possibly serve clinicians as a clinical decision support tool for clinicians to better understand treatment protocols and statistics done for patients. We have done ours with medication information & lab results with following patient group:

SNOMED ID #254837009: Malignant neoplasm of breast (disorder)
